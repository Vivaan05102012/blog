places must visit

fateh sagar lake

I have visited in December 2023.

Fateh Sagar Lake is a beautiful artificial lake located in the city of Udaipur, Rajasthan, India. It is surrounded by the Aravalli hills on three sides and Pratap Memorial on the north side. The lake is considered as the pride of Udaipur and is one of the four lakes of the city. The lake offers a serene and tranquil environment, making it an ideal spot for picnics, boating, and sightseeing. The lake is home to three tiny islands that can be reached by boat. The islands are a popular tourist attraction and offer many exciting activities. The lake is also a great place to witness the spectacular sunset over the Aravalli hills. The lake is surrounded by many cafes and restaurants that offer a variety of food and drinks. In summary, Fateh Sagar Lake is a must-visit destination in Udaipur, offering a peaceful and scenic environment, exciting activities, and delicious food.




india gate

I have visited in December 2023.

India Gate is a symbol of national pride and a prominent landmark in Delhi, India. Visiting it offers a chance to pay homage to the soldiers who sacrificed their lives in World War I. The iconic structure, designed by Sir Edwin Lutyens, showcases exquisite architecture and stands as a memorial to the Unknown Soldier. Surrounding lawns provide a serene atmosphere for reflection, making it a popular spot for both locals and tourists. The evening illumination adds a touch of grandeur, enhancing the overall experience.




taj mahal

I have visited in December 2023.

The Taj Mahal is an architectural masterpiece, renowned for its stunning white marble beauty. Its intricate design and symmetry showcase the pinnacle of Mughal artistry. Visiting allows you to witness a symbol of eternal love, as it was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. The historical and cultural significance, coupled with its breathtaking aesthetics, make it a must-see wonder of the world.




review on movies

Tiger Zinda Hai
Good action/thriller movie

I watched "Tiger Zinda Hai" for the first time in January 2022, and I ended up watching it 10 more times for several reasons. The intense and well-executed action sequences really grabbed my attention, making me crave those adrenaline-pumping moments over and over. The dynamic chemistry between Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif, coupled with their commendable performances, added a lot to the movie's rewatch value. The gripping plot, revolving around espionage and a mission to save hostages, kept me on the edge of my seat, making it worthwhile to revisit and catch any missed nuances. The emotional connection with the characters, their charisma, and relatable journey resonated with me on different levels. Lastly, the high production values, impressive cinematography, and captivating soundtrack created an immersive cinematic experience that I found irresistible, prompting multiple viewings.

Dabangg 3
Good action/thriller movie

I recently watched Dabangg 3 and found it to be a mixed bag of entertainment. Salman Khan's charismatic portrayal of Chulbul Pandey continues to be the film's strength, bringing his signature swagger and humor to the forefront. The action sequences are high-octane and visually appealing, showcasing Salman's prowess as an action hero. However, the film falters in its predictable storyline and lack of innovation. While the music adds a touch of nostalgia with the reprised version of some iconic tracks, overall, the soundtrack fails to leave a lasting impression. Dabangg 3 is a treat for Salman Khan fans who enjoy his larger-than-life persona, but it falls short in delivering a fresh and engaging narrative.

Tiger 3
Good action/thriller movie

"I watched Tiger 3 recently and I must say that it was a thrilling experience. The movie is directed by Maneesh Sharma and stars Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif in the lead roles. The story revolves around Tiger (Salman Khan) and his partner Zoya (Katrina Kaif) who face a personal threat when a vengeful terrorist from Pakistan issues an ultimatum. The movie is packed with constant thrills, plot twists, and high-octane action sequences. Salman Khan looks fresh and back in perfect form, performing dare-devil stunts that not only defy death and gravity but also logic. Katrina Kaif gets a well-defined character arc with a convincing backstory, a solid motive, and context. She pulls off an action-heavy role with ease, kicking some serious butt. The movie is a good addition to the franchise with enough ammo for mass entertainment. However, the dialogues lack the punch to evoke constant applause and seetis from Bhai fans. Despite such heavy dependence on VFX, ‘Tiger 3’ lacks the required finesse in many scenes, much like its jarring background score. Overall, I would recommend this movie to anyone who loves action-packed thrillers.
